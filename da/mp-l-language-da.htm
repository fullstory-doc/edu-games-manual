Language - Packs

Croation

There are three packages:

  1. Croatian Additional - installs simple environmental settings for languages and the debian maintainers guide - packages: language-env and maint-guide
  2. Croatian - installs croatian environment (KDE, man pages, spelling) - packages: kde-i18n-hr, aspell-hr, myspell-hr
  3. Croatian Openoffice - Croatian OpenOffice environment - Packages: openoffice.org-l10n-hr, openoffice.org-hyphenation-hr

Polish

There are three packages

  1. Language-Polish_Core - installs polish environment (KDE, man pages, spelling) - packages: kde-i18n-pl, aspell-pl, manpages-pl, myspell-pl, ipolish, k3b-i18n
  2. Language-Polish_Openoffice - Openoffice will start speaking polish - Packages: openoffice.org-l10n-pl, openoffice.org-hyphenation-pl, openoffice.org-thesaurus-pl
  3. Language-Polish_Icedove_Iceweasel-Polish GUI will warm ice - Packages icedove-locale-pl, iceweasel-l10n-pl

Greek

There are two packages:

  1. Greek - Installs the Greek environment for KDE, the Greek KDE manual, two spell checking applications for greek and the Greek sidux manual - packages: kde-i18n-el, aspell-el, myspell-el-gr, sidux-manual-el
  2. Greek Openoffice - Greek environment for OpenOffice - packages: openoffice.org-l10n-el

Portugese

There are two packages:

  1. Portugese - Installs the Portugese environment for KDE, the Portugese KDE manual, two spell checking applications for Portugese and the Portugese (pt-br) sidux manual - packages: kde-l10n-ptbr , aspell-pt-br , myspell-pt-br , sidux-manual-pt-br
  2. Portugese Openoffice - Portugese environment for OpenOffice - packages: openoffice.org-l10n-pt-br
